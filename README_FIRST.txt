   Spring Jam 26
================================================================================

HOW TO START THE GAME (EASIEST):

1. Look on your Desktop
2. Double-click the big icon called:
      "Tornado Shelter Sim"

3. A BLACK WINDOW will open and stay open.

WHAT TO DO IN THE BLACK WINDOW:

- It will try to find Godot automatically.
- If it finds Godot, it will open the project for you.
- The black window will stay open until you press a key at the end.

IF IT SAYS IT CANNOT FIND GODOT:

- It will give you clear instructions.
- You can DRAG your Godot.exe file and drop it directly into the black window.
- Then press Enter.
- It will remember the location for next time.

The black window will now wait for you and give you time to read.
